You may share or print your flashcards with a friend by clicking the export button while in the overview of a stack. This feature is exclusive to users with an active subscription.

Adding images is as simple as clicking on the image tool in the creator. Then select and scale an image from your gallery.

Rename a stack or folder by pressing on the edit button in the main overview of the app. Then while in edit mode you may tap a folder or stack to rename it.
